About Us

The Chefs are exceptionally skilled with more than 10 years of experience in various parts of the world. Using recipes passed down through generations, Durga Indian cuisine is set to become your new favourite local. We prepare our recipes by delicately balancing the spices and flavors in order to suit the taste palette of everyone.

Quality over profits is our Motto! We ensure our diners to get the best of taste and quality food that gives a sense of fulfillment and satisfaction to our diners. That makes us best authentic Indian | modern Indian food restaurant in Daisy hill, Shailer Park, Rochedale South, Logan City, Waterford West, Springwood, Woodridge, Beenleigh, Queensland, Brisbane, Australia.
